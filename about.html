About the business

Alan Snell is the founder and owner of Town & Country Autobody & Paint, a local business specializing in high-quality autobody and collision repair services. Alan has been in the autobody industry for almost 50 years, starting as a technician and working his way up to managing several shops for top dealerships in Spokane, WA. He decided to start his own business in 2001, with a vision of providing excellent customer service and craftsmanship. Since then, Town & Country Autobody has grown to become one of the most trusted and respected autobody shops in the area, serving customers from Spokane and south into the Palouse. Alan has employed as many as six employees at one time but currently works with two part-time assistants. He is proud of his achievements and his loyal clientele but is also looking forward to retiring soon and passing on his legacy to the next generation of autobody professionals. If you are looking for a reliable, honest, and experienced autobody shop, look no further than Town & Country Autobody & Paint.
